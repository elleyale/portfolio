–Where am I?

You've entered a space where physical and digital realities converge—where computational thinking meets hands-on making, where research questions lead to tangible systems.

This is my research practice: exploring how we capture, represent, and interact with the physical world through digital means. From scanning rigs that democratize 3D capture to virtual simulations that accelerate hardware design, each project investigates the evolving relationship between matter and data.

Here, technology is both tool and subject. Every system built, every pipeline designed, every workflow optimized is an inquiry into how we extend human presence into digital space—and how we bring digital intelligence into physical form.

Welcome to the laboratory. Welcome to the convergence.
