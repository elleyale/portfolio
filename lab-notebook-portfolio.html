DOPL: 87-Camera Volumetric Capture System

Design, Engineering, Deployment

Research Question

How can we democratize high-fidelity 3D scanning for consumer applications while maintaining professional-grade quality and reliability?

Approach

  • Designed modular camera array architecture for scalability and maintenance
  • Integrated active pattern projection system for enhanced depth sensing
  • Built custom calibration system for sub-millimeter accuracy
  • Developed lightweight material strategy for portability

Outcome

Consumer-grade volumetric capture system optimized for commercial deployment. Modular design enables rapid reconfiguration and cost-effective production. System achieves professional quality results with significantly reduced setup complexity.

Collaborators: Dopl team
Tools: CAD, Python, RealityCapture, custom electronics
2023

Virtual Scanner: Computational Approach to Hardware Design

Pre-visualization & Research

Hypothesis

Virtual camera placement testing can optimize physical rig design before costly manufacturing, reducing development cycles and material waste.

Method

  • Created virtual environment in Houdini for physics-accurate simulation
  • Simulated multiple camera array configurations with varying parameters
  • Analyzed coverage patterns, overlap zones, and blind spots
  • Rendered precise visualization for conceptualization and stakeholder review

Result

Fast-tracked development cycle by 40% through computational pre-validation. Enabled testing of configurations that would have been prohibitively expensive to prototype physically. Methodology now integrated into standard design workflow.

Tools: Houdini, Python, computational geometry
Contribution: Concept, research, implementation

–Where am I?

You've entered a space where physical and digital realities converge—where computational thinking meets hands-on making, where research questions lead to tangible systems.

This is my research practice: exploring how we capture, represent, and interact with the physical world through digital means. From scanning rigs that democratize 3D capture to virtual simulations that accelerate hardware design, each project investigates the evolving relationship between matter and data.

Here, technology is both tool and subject. Every system built, every pipeline designed, every workflow optimized is an inquiry into how we extend human presence into digital space—and how we bring digital intelligence into physical form.
